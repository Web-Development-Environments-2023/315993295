    • About me

    Hey, I'm Ozzy Osbourne, the Prince of Darkness! I've been rockin' since the late 1960s, and I'm still going strong today. I'm known for my wild stage antics, my distinctive vocals, and my unique sense of humor. I've had my share of ups and downs over the years, but I've always stayed true to my passion for music and my fans.

    I started out with Black Sabbath, and we changed the face of rock with our dark, heavy sound. But when I went solo, I got to really explore my own artistic vision. I've released some classic albums, like "Blizzard of Ozz," "Diary of a Madman," and "No More Tears." I've had some crazy experiences along the way, like biting the head off a bat on stage (it was an accident, I swear!) and getting fired from Black Sabbath for being too out of control.

    Despite all the craziness, though, I'm proud of my legacy and my impact on rock music.
